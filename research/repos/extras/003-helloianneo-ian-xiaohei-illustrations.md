## Key Ideas

- **Xiaohei (小黑) IP character** — distinctive black solid character with white dot eyes and thin legs; visual anchor in every illustration
- **16:9 horizontal format** — optimized for Chinese article body illustrations; white background, hand-drawn line art, generous whitespace
- **Limited color annotations** — sparing use of red, orange, blue Chinese handwritten annotations for emphasis only
- **Cognitive anchor mapping** — analyzes articles to identify key cognitive points, judgments, processes, structures, or metaphors to visualize
- **Shot list generation** — auto-produces 4-8 image shot lists per article with theme, core meaning, structure type, and Xiaohei action
- **8 visual structure types** — Workflow, System Detail, Before/After Comparison, Character State, Concept Metaphor, Method Layering, Map Route, Comic Strip panels
- **QA checklist** — white background, generous whitespace, Xiaohei participation, Chinese annotation correctness, anti-PPT-feel
- **Low-tech physical metaphor** — each illustration invents a low-tech, absurd but plausible physical metaphor for the concept
